Frequently Asked Questions

What is "In a Box"?
In a Box is a company that delivers food boxes to your home. We provide a range of services and products, including recipe guides, bake-at-home sets, ingredients, meal planners, a nutritional information app, a selection of weekly recipe choices, and gift boxes for special occasions.
How can I contact your customer support team?
You can reach our friendly customer support team by clicking on the "Contact Us" link in the website's footer. Alternatively, you can send an email to inaboxorg@email.com or call our toll-free hotline at +12018577757.
Do you offer gift options for special occasions?
Yes, we do! We offer special occasion gift boxes that are perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or any other celebration. You can browse our selection and add a personalized message during checkout to make the gift extra special.
Is your website accessible for all users?
Yes, we are committed to ensuring our website is accessible to a wide range of users. We have implemented accessibility features to make it easier for everyone, including those with disabilities, to use our site easily and comfortably.
Do you offer cooking tips or tutorials with your recipes?
Yes, each "In A Box" comes with a detailed recipe guide that includes step-by-step instructions and cooking tips to make your meal preparation a breeze.
